Sept 29th, 2015
'Student's Offering Support (SOS)' Training Session

On September 29th, it was our honor to be asked to help train this year's SOS captains in public speaking. For anyone who doesn't know what SOS is about, they're an awesome social venture that provides students with midterm and finals preparations. Money is raised by holding group review sessions headed by volunteer senior students. The funds gathered from these events are then used to help build schools in countries across the world. This is a live snippet from the event.

June 30th, 2015
Pablo Milandu & Focus on Youth 2015

On June 30th, 2015 - the Golden Speakers Club was invited to speak in front of students who were about to enter into their very first jobs. Pablo's purpose was to teach Financial Literacy to more than 100 teenagers.
They are now equipped with the necessary tools and knowledge to embark on a path of wealth.
As always, it is our pleasure to make a lasting difference in the lives of young people. It's something we like to call Inspiration Through Speech

June 5th, 2015
Golden Speakers & Focus on Youth 2015
June 5th to June 7th, that weekend marks the Golden Speakers Club's very 1st Speaking Engagement outside of Laurier.
Mike Consul, Andre Fullerton, Greg Rogers, and the rest of their team invited us to Camp Olympia to speak to their students about how to make the most out of their conversations and how to find their passion. Let me tell you this: we came in determined to inspire these young leaders but I can honestly say that we left as the ones inspired. Talking to these students, we only felt love, kindness, and energy. Lots of energy. Are mosquitoes attracted to that? It was obvious that they wanted to be there that weekend and they do really want to make a difference in children's lives. They will be remembered for the positive impact that they will make in their community and beyond.
Young leaders sharing stories and creating opportunities that will last a lifetime. It's something we like to call Inspiration Through Speech.
